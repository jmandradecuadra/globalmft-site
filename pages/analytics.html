Business analytics consulting enables organizations to leverage data for improved decision-making, operational efficiency, and strategic growth. Our services span data strategy, implementation of analytics platforms including AI and ML, data visualization, and performance management across marketing, finance, and operations departments.

We work with clients to build sustainable data capabilities — not just deliver a dashboard. Every engagement includes knowledge transfer and enablement so your team can operate the analytics environment independently.

Core Services

Analytics Consulting Capabilities

01

🗂️ Data Strategy & Planning

We develop AI-ready data strategies, governance frameworks, and analytics roadmaps that align your data investments with business priorities. This includes defining your data ownership model, quality standards, and the architecture needed to support advanced analytics.

  • AI strategy & data governance frameworks
  • Analytics maturity assessment
  • Data architecture & platform selection
  • Data quality management programs
  • Analytics COE (Center of Excellence) setup
02

🔗 Data Implementation & Integration

We build and migrate data platforms, integrating disparate data sources into unified, analytics-ready environments. Whether standing up a modern data warehouse or connecting your ERP to a BI tool, we handle the full data engineering lifecycle.

  • Data warehouse & data lake implementation
  • ETL/ELT pipeline development
  • BI tool integration (Power BI, Tableau, Looker)
  • API and data connector development
  • Legacy data migration
03

🤖 Advanced Analytics & AI

We apply predictive modeling, machine learning, and AI-powered dashboards to help organizations forecast demand, detect anomalies, and surface insights that traditional reporting misses. We deploy models that are explainable, auditable, and maintainable by your team.

  • Predictive & prescriptive analytics
  • Machine learning model development
  • Natural language processing (NLP)
  • AI-powered forecasting dashboards
  • Model monitoring & retraining pipelines
04

📊 Data Visualization & Reporting

We create executive dashboards, operational reports, and self-service analytics environments that track KPIs, financial indicators, and marketing effectiveness. Our visualizations are designed for clarity and action — not just aesthetics.

  • Executive KPI dashboards
  • Financial performance reporting
  • Marketing attribution dashboards
  • Self-service BI portal setup
  • Mobile-ready reporting
05

🏭 Operational & Functional Analytics

We apply analytics to optimize core business functions — workforce management, inventory, supply chain, and customer operations. These engagements connect data directly to operational decisions and measurable efficiency gains.

  • Supply chain & inventory analytics
  • Workforce analytics & planning
  • Customer journey analytics
  • Operational KPI tracking
  • Root cause analysis frameworks
06

🎓 Advisory & Training

We offer expert advisory, hands-on training, and ongoing support to accelerate data adoption across your organization. This includes data literacy programs for business users and technical training for data teams.

  • Data literacy workshops
  • Analytics tool training (Power BI, Tableau)
  • Ongoing advisory retainers
  • Analytics team coaching
  • Documentation & knowledge transfer

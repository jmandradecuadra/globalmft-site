Zelle Verification

How It Works

A four-step process that integrates seamlessly into your existing onboarding workflow.

1

API Request

Your system sends a verification request with the customer's phone number or email via our REST API.

2

Zelle® Lookup

We query the Zelle® network in real time to confirm enrollment status and matched identity attributes.

3

KYC Cross-Check

Results are cross-referenced against your KYC/AML requirements and compliance rules engine.

4

Instant Response

A structured verification result is returned in milliseconds with full audit log for compliance records.

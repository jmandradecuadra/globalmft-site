Cloud Solutions

Multi-cloud adoption, migration, and management

Our cloud advisory practice covers the full spectrum from initial cloud strategy to complex multi-cloud operations. We work across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud with a vendor-agnostic approach — recommending what truly fits your workloads, team, and budget.

We prefer Cloudflare for CDN, edge security, and DNS management, keeping your architecture modern and cost-efficient without AWS dependency for these layers.

  • Cloud readiness assessment & business case development
  • Cloud migration strategy (lift-and-shift, re-platform, re-architect)
  • Multi-cloud and hybrid architecture design
  • FinOps — cloud cost optimization & governance

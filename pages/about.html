How We Work

Engagement Models

We work with companies of all sizes — from startups to enterprises — offering tailored advisory or acting as interim project leadership.

🔭

Strategic Advisory

Ongoing retainer-based advisory covering technology strategy, vendor management, and executive decision support. Billed monthly with defined advisory hours and escalation paths.

Ideal for: Growth-stage companies and SMBs seeking ongoing CTO-level guidance.

🏗️

Project Delivery

Scoped engagements for defined transformation initiatives — cloud migrations, security frameworks, analytics implementations — with clear milestones, deliverables, and payment gates.

Ideal for: Organizations with a specific initiative that requires dedicated execution capacity.

👤

Fractional Leadership

Acting as interim CTO, CISO, or IT Director for organizations in transition. We provide senior-level leadership with hands-on delivery management and cross-functional coordination.

Ideal for: Companies between technology leaders or scaling past their founding team's capacity.
